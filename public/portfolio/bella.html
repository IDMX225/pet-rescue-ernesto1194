Meet Bella

Breed:

Age:

3 Years

Gender:

Female

Color:

Bella is a 3-year-old Siamese with beautiful seal-point coloring and striking blue eyes. She’s an affectionate and chatty cat, known for her playful antics and sweet nature. Bella loves attention and will often follow you around the house to make sure you're never too far away.

With her outgoing personality, Bella thrives in an active home where she can interact with her humans and enjoy playtime. She’s always up for a game of chase or a fun toy to bat around. Bella is also very vocal and will let you know when it’s time for treats or snuggles.

If you're looking for a lively, loving companion with a bit of sass and a lot of charm, Bella could be the perfect fit for your home.
