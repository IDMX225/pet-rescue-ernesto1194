Dusty is a 5-year-old Russian Blue with a soft, silvery-blue coat. Shy at first, he warms up quickly to those who earn his trust, and once comfortable, he’s affectionate and loves to cuddle. Dusty thrives in a peaceful, loving home and enjoys relaxing with his people. If you're looking for a beautiful, loyal companion who enjoys both cuddles and personal space, Dusty could be the perfect match for you!

Social and affectionate, Dusty enjoys being around his humans and will happily sit by your side for attention. He’s a bit shy at first but once he trusts you, he’s a cuddler. Dusty loves to curl up next to you and relax in a peaceful environment.

Dusty would thrive in a home that appreciates a calm and loving companion. If you're looking for a sweet-natured cat to keep you company, Dusty is ready to meet you!
